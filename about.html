Apartments Srdarović A Perfect Seaside Getaway

Welcome to Apartments Srdarović, the ideal place for your seaside vacation! Our modernly furnished apartments are located in the beautiful village of Drage, right by the sea, where you can enjoy crystal-clear waters and breathtaking sunsets.

Newly renovated apartments with elegant interiors provide comfort and luxury throughout your stay. Spacious terraces offer stunning sea views, perfect for enjoying your morning coffee or relaxing with a glass of wine in the evening. Fully equipped kitchens allow you to prepare your favorite meals, and each apartment features air conditioning, Wi-Fi, TV, and all the essentials for a carefree stay. Beachfront location just a few steps from the apartments, you can enjoy swimming and sunbathing on beautiful beaches. Private parking is available for all guests.

Apartments Srdarović is a family-run business, where every member of the family is dedicated to making your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. With a warm and welcoming atmosphere, we strive to provide our guests with an unforgettable vacation experience.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat in nature or an adventure exploring nearby attractions like Lake Vrana, Kornati National Park, Šibenik, Zadar and Biograd na Moru, Apartments Srdarović are the perfect choice for you!
