Vransko lake and Vrana


Old Town of Vrana (Settlement and Gradina Site) is a historical locality in the village of Vrana, situated near the shores of Lake Vrana and on the border of the Vrana Lake Nature Park. This medieval fortress served religious, military, and civil purposes.The Town of Vrana holds exceptional significance in Croatian medieval history as a center of great ecclesiastical and secular power from the 11th to the 14th century. It was also the custodian of the Croatian royal crown and other priceless treasures.


The construction of Mašković Han began in 1644 by the order and funds of Jusuf Mašković, originally from Vrana, a high dignitary on the Sultan's court and a supreme admiral of the Turkish fleet.


Near the highest peak of the Nature Park Vransko Lake (Štandarac, 303 m.a.s.l.), there is the most attractive viewpoint of this area Info centre Kamenjak, at a height of about 283 meters above the sea.In the area of the info centre you can get more information about the Nature Park, buy souvenirs with motifs of Vransko Lake, cycling and excursion tickets, and you can also enjoy the specialties of the traditional tavern. In mid-September, meet local farmers at the “Luka i igara” fair.


Nature Park Vransko Lake represents a place of direct encounter with nature and offers a unique learning opportunity. Find out about the little secret of a big pond, what species of plant is harder than stone, who are the stars of Vransko Lake and why we need to be wise with wetlands! Get to know exceptional biological diversity and rich cultural heritage through the thematic educational paths that run along the entire Nature Park.


In a unique area, close to the info centre Crkvine, in a pine forest, there is an adventure park where you can indulge in adventure and test your skills. Walking on wooden mushrooms, hanging bridges and nets, climbing on artificial rock and going down a zipline will awaken the adventurer in you.
